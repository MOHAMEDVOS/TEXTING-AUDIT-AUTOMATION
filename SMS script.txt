📲 SMS SELLER SCRIPT 
________________


INITIAL OUTREACH (The automated text)
Text Sent (WF Entry Point):
 "Hey , I'd love to chat about opportunities for your property at . I'm available when you are. — Jack with LHB"
________________


 IF THEY REPLY WITH INTEREST OR QUESTIONS
Common Responses:
* “What’s this about?”

* “Who is this?”

* “Why are you texting me?”

* “Yes” or any soft interest

Response:
 "Thanks for getting back to me. Texting you regarding your property at <address>,  do you have any plans to sell it soon or down the road?"
________________


 IF THEY REPLY “NO” TO INITIAL TEXT
First Rebuttal:
 "No worries, I was just wondering if you would like to sell ."
Second Rebuttal (if they still say no):
 "Not a problem at all. We can start the process whenever you are ready. We're very flexible on timeline. Do you think it could be for sale within the next 6 months?"
Final Rebuttal (if still no):
 "Understood. Do you have anything, or know someone who wants to maybe sell? I usually look for homes that need some work. I pay $1,000 for referrals I close on."
(Stop texting — mark as Not Interested)
________________


IF THEY SHOW INTEREST
   * Check if the property is a duplicate or listed
→ If listed: mark as listed
 WIDE FUNNEL (WF)
      * Push the Lead
________________


 MEDIUM FUNNEL (MF)
Ask for motivation:
 "If you don't mind me asking, could you share a bit about the reason for selling? I completely understand if it's personal."
→ If valid motivation is provided: Push Lead
 Note: If vague or no reason, we can try by going to the Narrow funnel ( To collect more info)
________________


 NARROW FUNNEL (NF)
1. Ask about the condition:
 "I can get an offer over to you! I just want to confirm, I'm quickly checking online and it looks like this is a # Beds and # Baths home. Is that correct? And have you done any repairs in the last 4 to 5 years?"
2. Ask about asking price:
      * If no major updates:
"Got it, thanks for letting me know! If you could sell your home as-is for a cash deal and we covered all of the closing costs and fees, where do you think you'd need to be on price?"


         * If updated:
"Oh, that's great! Sounds like you've put a lot of work into the place. If you could sell your home as-is for a cash deal and we covered all of the closing costs and fees, where do you think you'd need to be on price?"
Evaluate asking price:
            * If above market:
"I appreciate the reply, but that is going to be more than I can pay. If by chance you know anyone looking to sell, I pay $1,000 on all referrals I close on!" (End conversation)

            * If reasonable/below market:
 Move to the next question:
3. Ask about the motivation and the closing time
 "Okay, that sounds good. Also, I'm curious, what is it that's making you consider a deal right now? And what kind of timeline would you have in mind for closing?"

If only motivation is given:
"Got it, do you think we can do the closing within 6 months?"
→ If answered: Push Lead
________________


 FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCES (If No Response)
First follow-up: Later in the same day
Second follow-up: After 2 days from the last follow-up
Third follow-up: After 2 days from the last follow-up

 SPECIAL CASE RESPONSES
 "Wrong Number"
"I'm so sorry about that! Would you by chance know someone who may want or need to sell their home? We pay up to $1,000 for any referral you bring to us!"
"Call Me"
"Would love to but, before we hop on a call, can you tell me a bit more about the house? I would hate to waste your time if it's not the right fit."
If they insist:
 "Totally understandable! When would be a good time for a quick call? I’ll make sure to reach out then."
 Bluffing
Don’t push. Label as a Bluffer and move on. If unsure, check with TL.
