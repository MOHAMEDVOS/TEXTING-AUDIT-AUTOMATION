01 — The Three Funnels

Every Chat Is in One of These Funnels

Wide Funnel (WF)

The Hello

The goal is to get a reply. The system checks if the agent is warm and not giving up too early. No big pillars needed yet.

Rules Checked

  • Rude Tone
  • Giving Up After 1 No
  • Rebuttal Used
  • Lead Said STOP or Remove Me
  • Label Match

Middle Funnel (MF)

The Nurture

The goal is to learn a little. The system checks if the agent gathered 1 or 2 pillars — like why the lead is selling or their timeline.

Rules Checked

  • 1–2 Pillars Gathered
  • Rude Tone
  • Rebuttal Used
  • Lead Said STOP or Remove Me
  • Label Match

Narrow Funnel (NF)

The Qualify

The goal is full facts. All 4 pillars must be complete before a handoff msg is sent to the client.

Rules Checked

  • All 4 Pillars Gathered
  • Handoff Msg Sent
  • Rude Tone
  • Rebuttal Used
  • Label Match

03 — Grading Pipeline

Four Levels Check Every Chat

What is ML?

ML looks at thousands of past conversations and learns what a "clean" or "mistake" chat looks like — then it makes a decision on its own. Levels 1, 2, and 3 below use ML.

1

Quick Search ML

Scans for clear STOP words like "Remove me" or "Wrong Number."

Instant Pass
2

Memory Search ML

Compares the chat to past conversations that were already confirmed "Clean."

Auto Pass
3

Smart Guess ML

A small model that predicts whether any mistake is likely in this chat.

Auto Pass
4

AI MODEL

Connects to the AI model for complex decisions. Reads every word to be 100% sure.

Deep Review

04 — Current Limits

What the ML Can and Cannot Do Now

Level 1–3 are a cost-saving filter. Their job is to skip easy chats — not to audit mistakes.

Level What it detects now Current limit
Level 01 Clear STOP words like "Remove me" or "Wrong Number" Soft or unclear rejections
Level 02 Exact matches to past "Clean" conversations Chats it has never seen before
Level 03 Whether a mistake is likely at all Cannot name the specific mistake
Level 04 Full audit — tone, pillars, label, rebuttal Cost per call — reserved for hard cases
Training Plan

Real data is being collected to teach the system how to catch mistakes automatically — making it smarter and cheaper over time.

Does the ML mark mistakes now?

No. Right now, Level 1–3 only validates the "Good" chats. It does not mark mistakes. If a mistake is spotted, the chat is sent to Level 4 (AI MODEL) to confirm it and create the Red Flag report. This is done to be 100% sure before giving an agent a bad grade.
